Prediction using XAI

Explainable AI (XAI) refers to methods and techniques in artificial intelligence that make the decisions or predictions of AI models transparent, understandable, and interpretable to humans. Unlike traditional "black-box" models, where the internal workings are opaque, XAI aims to provide insights into how and why a model arrives at a specific output. This is crucial for building trust, ensuring fairness, debugging models, and meeting regulatory requirements in domains like healthcare, finance, and law.

Key Goals of XAI

  • Transparency: Clarifying how the model processes inputs to produce outputs.
  • Interpretability: Enabling humans to understand the reasoning behind predictions.
  • Accountability: Ensuring decisions can be audited or justified.
